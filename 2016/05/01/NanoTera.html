    My Thesis in 180s

    In 2016, I participated in an internal "My Thesis in 180s" organized by Nano-Tera.ch, a Swiss initiative that founded multiple cutting-edge research projects, including X-Sense2, in which my group was involved.

    This performance tells the story of the PermaSense project, which deploys wireless sensor networks in the mountains to enable fundamental geology and glaciology research... and for early warning!
